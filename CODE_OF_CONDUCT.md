# ü§ù Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior
- **Use welcoming and inclusive language**
- **Respect different viewpoints and experiences**
- **Accept constructive criticism gracefully**
- **Focus on what is best for the community**
- **Show empathy towards other community members**

### Unacceptable Behavior
- **Harassment, discriminatory language, or imagery**
- **Personal attacks, political attacks, or insults**
- **Public or private harassment**
- **Publishing private information without permission**
- **Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## üéØ Our Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers
- **Clarify standards**: Make expectations clear and accessible
- **Enforce standards**: Enforce the code of conduct fairly and consistently
- **Respond appropriately**: Respond to reports in a timely and appropriate manner
- **Respect privacy**: Respect the privacy and safety of those reporting incidents

### Contributors
- **Follow standards**: Follow the code of conduct in all interactions
- **Report issues**: Report harassment or inappropriate behavior to maintainers
- **Be professional**: Maintain professional conduct in all community interactions
- **Be inclusive**: Help create an inclusive environment for all participants

## üö® Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it through one of these channels:

1. **Email**: conduct@aiappsy.com
2. **GitHub Issues**: [Create a report](https://github.com/your-repo/issues/new?template=security_report)
3. **Private Message**: Send a private message to any project maintainer

### What to Include
- **Your contact information**: So we can follow up
- **Incident description**: What happened, when, and where
- **Individuals involved**: Who was affected or witnessed
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, logs, or other documentation
- **Desired outcome**: What you would like to see happen

### Response Process
- **Acknowledgment**: Within 24 hours
- **Investigation**: Thorough review of the incident
- **Resolution**: Appropriate action based on findings
- **Follow-up**: Communication about resolution

## üîß Enforcement

### Consequences
Unacceptable behavior from any community member will not be tolerated. Depending on the severity of the behavior, project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including but not limited to:

- **Warning**: Private warning about inappropriate behavior
- **Temporary Ban**: Temporary removal from community spaces
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from community
- **Legal Action**: Involvement of law enforcement if necessary

### Appeals Process
If you feel you have been unfairly accused or penalized:
1. **Submit appeal**: Send a detailed appeal to conduct@aiappsy.com
2. **Review process**: Different maintainers will review the case
3. **Decision**: Final decision will be communicated within 7 days
4. **Resolution**: Decision will be enforced by project maintainers
